# Finova
Finova is a full-stack personal finance web app built by Team The Visionaries during our industry training at Sumago Infotech Pvt. Ltd. What started as a simple idea — "what if managing money was actually easy?" — turned into a fully deployed, AI-powered application that anyone can use from any device, anywhere in the world.

✨ Features
🔐 Secure Login & Registration — Every user gets their own private account. Your data is yours and yours alone.
💵 Log Income & Expenses — Add transactions in seconds with categories, dates and descriptions.
🗑️ Delete Transactions — Made a mistake? Clean it up easily.
📊 Visual Financial Reports — See your money through beautiful pie charts, area charts and bar graphs.
📅 Monthly Trends — Understand how your spending changes month by month.
🗂️ Category Wise Breakdown — Know exactly how much you spend on food, transport, shopping and more.
🤖 FinBot — AI Money Assistant — Chat with FinBot and get personalized budget tips based on your actual transactions.

| Technology | Purpose |
